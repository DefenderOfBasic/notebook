A foundational principle of open memetics is: just ask for people's data. People will happily give you their data if they understand why you want it, and feel like it will be good for them. 

### Examples of "just ask"

- Washington Post collecting thousands of people's TikTok archives by just telling them they want to analyze them: https://thewashingtonpost.formstack.com/forms/help_investigate_tiktoks_algorithm
